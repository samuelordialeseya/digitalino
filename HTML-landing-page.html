WHAT IS DigiTALINO?

(h)over here!

DigiTALINO is a user-friendly website that teaches people how to acquire critical digital literacy skills and strong cyber-safety behaviors. It offers clear, practical guidance on how to recognize online threats, keep oneself safe, and use technology sensibly. DigiTALINO uses interactive technology and user-friendly courses to teach individuals of all ages how to confidently navigate the digital world. DigiTALINO is a dependable partner on your digital adventure, regardless of your degree of expertise.
