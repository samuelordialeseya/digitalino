Definition of Terms

By Pauline Bernadette Diaz

What is Digital Literacy?

DIGITAL LITERACY is the ability to effectively use, and navigate digital tools such as computers, smartphones, internet, and various software applications. This includes understanding how to find, evaluate, and utilize information online, how to communicate and collaborate through digital means.

What is Cyber Safety?

CYBERSECURITY is the technical protection of systems and networks from theft or damage.

Meanwhile, CYBER SAFETY is the practice, measure, and behavior of protecting individuals, organizations, and electronic data from possible harm in the digital environment. This concerns a wider human-focused approach such as responsible online behavior.

Other Common Terms:

  • MEDIA LITERACY is the ability to critically read information, and utilize media to creatively produce communication.
  • INFORMATION LITERACY is the ability to find credible information, evaluate its validity, and use it correctly. This involves verifying data before sharing them.
  • ICT LITERACY is the ability to know how to select and use digital technologies to finish tasks that require the use of the internet.
  • NETIQUETTE is the rules of conduct to respectfully and appropriately communicate online like using polite language, and respecting the privacy of others.
  • IDENTITY MANAGEMENT is being able to understand how to ensure safety and security in managing online identity, and foster positive digital reputation.

Importance of Digital Literacy

Digital Literacy has become an essential skill in today's digital world, influencing various aspects of daily life. Individuals must possess an understanding of how to navigate the digital landscape, critically evaluate information, and protect themselves from possible threats. It plays a role in facilitating access to information, empowering individuals to effectively search for, assess, and utilize the information to pursue opportunities for personal and career growth.

Conclusion

Digital Literacy is essentially important from enhancing access to information, and facilitating communication to promoting online safety. Therefore, digital literacy profoundly influences the various aspects of contemporary life.
