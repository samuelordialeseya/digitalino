Is your answer "Say NO and tell a trusted adult"?

✔️Then you chose the right option!

Meeting a stranger who offers gifts, especially something unrealistic like a brand-new car — is a major online grooming and scam warning sign. Strangers often use bribery, flattery, or fake prizes to manipulate someone into meeting in person.

WHAT ARE THE SAFEST OPTION TO DO?

  • Do NOT engage further with the other party. They will take this as a chance to manipulate you further.

  • If the situation has escalated, contact the authorities and report the incident. Always prioritize you and your family's privacy and safety. Even when it hasn't escalated, contact the authorities and have them arrested for cyber-crime.

  • Tell a TRUSTED adult and let them handle the situation.

The given list above is the three most likely actions to do when put in a situation where you have not been hacked/scammed by the other party, but ALSO AFTER you have been hacked/scammed. Any other actions that need to be taken will be handled either by you or the authorities.
